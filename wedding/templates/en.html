The Ceremony

Basilica Cattedrale di Trani, Trani, Puglia


Right on the coast, this beautiful Cathedral will host our wedding.


The ceremony will begin at 4.30pm. Guests are requested to arrive 30 minutes before the ceremony.


Parking is normally available outside the Cathedral. A bus will be available to transport those in hotels and Lavello to the cathedral and the reception venue.


Google Maps

The Reception

Villa Sant’Elia, Andria-Corato, Puglia


For those of you who haven't experienced at Italian wedding before, you're in for a treat.


There will be a lot of food, so don't fill up during the day :) There is also a free bar, so no money is required!


Festivities will go on in to the wee hours and there will be buses to take you back to your respective hotels.


Villa Sant’Elia Google Maps

Getting There

The wedding and reception is in the Puglia region of Italy. Famous for wine and sun :)


The closest airport is in Bari which is ~45 minutes away from the wedding venue in Trani. Bari is served by Ryanair from Dublin. From London, you can travel with Alitalia and Ryanair. Hipmunk is a good site for finding the best deals.


If you fly to Rome or Naples, it would be best to rent a car and drive to Puglia. It takes about 4 hours from Rome and 2.5 hours from Naples.

Staying There

Hotels are available in Trani, as are B&Bs (and airbnb). The nearby town of Barletta also has various hotels. We have reserved some rooms at the Nicotel in Barletta. Contact us if you'd like to stay there!


We've collected a few (not an exhaustive list) hotels and B&Bs for you. Trani is a really beautiful place, so it's worth staying in. Barletta has a larger hotel but has some equally beautiful surroundings of its own.
